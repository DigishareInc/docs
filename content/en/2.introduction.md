## What is Digishare?

Digishare is a powerful customer engagement and marketing platform. Our ecosystem allows you to manage campaigns, support tickets, contact lists, and real-time communications through a unified API.

## Key Features

- **Unified API**: Access all Digishare features through a single secure API.
- **Real-time Webhooks**: Get notified instantly about important events.
- **Campaign Management**: Orchestrate marketing flows across channels.
- **Ticketing System**: Integrated customer support solution.
