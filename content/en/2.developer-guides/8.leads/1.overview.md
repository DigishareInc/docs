Digishare's lead management system is designed to seamlessly bridge the gap between social media advertising and your internal sales workflows. 

## Key Benefits
- **Multi-channel integration**: Capture leads directly from Meta (Facebook/Instagram), TikTok Ads, and custom landing pages.
- **Normalization Engine**: Automatically maps and cleans up field data (names, phone numbers, emails) regardless of the original language or formatting.
- **Zero data loss**: Unrecognized fields are safely stored as custom attributes, ensuring every piece of customer information is preserved.
